What it is about?

Experience the rich culture and beauty of Puebla with our 7-day tour. This comprehensive package includes transportation services with a driver, translator, and guide to ensure a hassle-free experience. Discover the ancient pyramids of Cholula and Teotihuacan, explore 50 highlights of Puebla, and witness the wonders of Africam Safari. Immerse yourself in the charm of Magical Towns, indulge in a Gastronomic Tour featuring over 20 delicious dishes, and savor a wine tasting experience. Take part in a Mexican Ethnic Culture Tour and delve into the myths and legends of Puebla while visiting museums and famous sites. Throughout the tour, enjoy the company of an experienced translator and historian who will provide valuable insights and make your journey unforgettable. Please note that meals, drinks, and lodging are not included, but transportation from CDMX or Puebla airport to your Puebla hotel is provided. Embark on this complete Puebla experience and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of the city.
